# Deskorelax Product Context

Register: brand

Deskorelax is a public brand website for a windsurfing and sailing school in Kretowiny. Visitors are families, adults, returning guests, and people comparing summer water-sport options before contacting the team or opening an offer PDF.

The brand should feel warm, capable, direct, and lake-born. It is relaxed without looking casual or improvised. The website should sell the atmosphere of a real place: wind, sun, instructors, boards, boats, shallow water, camps, and easy contact.

Primary actions:
- Open the offer.
- Contact the team.
- Understand the spot and atmosphere quickly.
- Browse gallery/Instagram for proof of life.

Design principles:
- Real imagery first. Do not replace the lake with abstract decoration.
- Keep Deskorelax recognizable: blue water accents, warm summer light, rounded editorial surfaces, Polish copy.
- Make the site feel premium through composition, motion, hierarchy, and responsiveness, not through excessive effects.
- Animation should feel like wind and lake light: visible in the hero and page flow, quiet in repeated interactions.

Anti-goals:
- Generic SaaS landing page.
- Overly dark surf brand.
- Decorative glass/orb effects.
- Purely static brochure feel.
- Fake Instagram content or backend behavior.
